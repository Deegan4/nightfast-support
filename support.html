Frequently Asked Questions

üöÄ Getting Started

How do I sign in to NightFast?

‚ñº

NightFast uses Sign in with Apple for secure, private authentication. When you first open the app, tap "Sign in with Apple" and follow the prompts. You can choose to share or hide your email address - either option works perfectly.

How do I set my night hours?

‚ñº

Go to Settings > Night Schedule and set your preferred night start and end times. These hours are used for the Night Unlock Prompt feature and for categorizing your eating patterns.

What's the Night Unlock Prompt?

‚ñº

The Night Unlock Prompt is NightFast's signature feature. During your configured night hours, when you open your phone, you'll see a gentle prompt asking "Looking for a midnight snack?" You can quickly log food, get recommendations, or dismiss it. Configure frequency in Settings > Night Prompt.

üçé Food Logging

How do I log food quickly?

‚ñº

NightFast offers several quick logging options:

  • Quick Log: Tap the + button and choose Light/Medium/Heavy snack
  • Favorites: Foods you log often appear at the top
  • Barcode: Scan packaged foods for instant nutrition info
  • Photo: Take a photo and let AI identify the food
  • Siri: Say "Hey Siri, log food in NightFast"

Why can't I find a food in the database?

‚ñº

Our database includes millions of foods, but some items may not be available. Try:

  • Searching for a similar food
  • Using the barcode scanner if it's a packaged product
  • Entering the nutrition information manually

If a food is consistently missing, let us know at feedback@nightfast.app

How accurate is the photo food recognition?

‚ñº

Our AI can identify many common foods, but accuracy varies. It works best with:

  • Single foods on a plate
  • Good lighting conditions
  • Common dishes and ingredients

Always review and adjust the suggested nutrition values before confirming.

üí≥ Subscriptions & Billing

How do I cancel my subscription?

‚ñº

To cancel your subscription:

  1. Open the Settings app on your iPhone
  2. Tap your name at the top
  3. Tap Subscriptions
  4. Find and tap NightFast
  5. Tap Cancel Subscription

You'll continue to have access until the end of your billing period.

How do I restore my purchases?

‚ñº

If you've previously purchased NightFast+ or Pro and it's not showing:

  1. Open NightFast
  2. Go to Settings > Subscription
  3. Tap "Restore Purchases"

Make sure you're signed in with the same Apple ID used for the original purchase.

How does Family Sharing work?

‚ñº

NightFast Pro includes Family Sharing for up to 5 family members. Once you subscribe:

  1. Set up Family Sharing in your Apple ID settings (if not already done)
  2. Family members can download NightFast and sign in
  3. Pro features will be automatically available to all family members

Each family member has their own separate data and account.

Why isn't my data syncing between devices?

‚ñº

NightFast uses iCloud to sync your data. If sync isn't working:

  1. Ensure you're signed in to iCloud on all devices
  2. Check that iCloud Drive is enabled in Settings > [Your Name] > iCloud
  3. Make sure NightFast is enabled in iCloud (Settings > [Your Name] > iCloud > Apps Using iCloud)
  4. Wait a few minutes - initial sync can take time
  5. Try closing and reopening the app

How do I export my data?

‚ñº

To export your data:

  1. Go to Settings > Privacy > Export My Data
  2. Choose the date range and format (JSON or CSV)
  3. Tap Export
  4. Share or save the file

Your export includes all food logs, schedules, and preferences.

How do I delete my account and data?

‚ñº

To permanently delete your account:

  1. Go to Settings > Account > Delete Account
  2. Review what will be deleted
  3. Type "DELETE" to confirm
  4. Tap Delete My Account

Warning: This action cannot be undone. All your data will be permanently removed within 30 days.

‚ñº

To add NightFast widgets:

Available widgets: Daily Progress, Next Meal, Quick Log

Troubleshooting Common Issues

App Crashes or Won't Open

  1. Force quit the app by swiping up from the app switcher
  2. Restart your iPhone
  3. Check for app updates in the App Store
  4. Ensure you have enough storage space
  5. If problems persist, try deleting and reinstalling the app (your data is safe in iCloud)

HealthKit Not Syncing

  1. Go to Settings > Privacy & Security > Health
  2. Find NightFast and ensure all permissions are enabled
  3. Open the Health app and check if data appears
  4. In NightFast, go to Settings > Health and toggle sync off and on

Notifications Not Working

  1. Go to Settings > Notifications > NightFast
  2. Ensure "Allow Notifications" is enabled
  3. Check that the alert style, sounds, and badges are configured
  4. Make sure Do Not Disturb or Focus mode isn't blocking notifications
  5. In NightFast, verify notification settings in Settings > Notifications
