Privacy Policy

Last Updated: December 27, 2024

Summary: NightFast collects nutrition and health data to help you track your eating habits. Your data is stored securely in your personal iCloud account and is never sold to third parties.

1. Introduction

Welcome to NightFast ("we," "our," or "us"). We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring you understand how your information is collected, used, and protected when you use the NightFast application ("App").

This Privacy Policy applies to all users of the NightFast App available on the Apple App Store.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Information You Provide

  • Account Information: When you sign in with Apple, we receive your Apple ID email address (or a private relay email) and display name.
  • Nutrition Data: Food logs, calorie counts, macronutrient information, meal schedules, and dietary preferences you enter.
  • Profile Information: Daily calorie goals, dietary restrictions, night schedule preferences, and notification settings.
  • Photos: If you use the food recognition feature, photos are processed on-device and are not stored or transmitted.

2.2 Information Collected Automatically

  • Usage Data: App interactions, feature usage, and session information for improving the App.
  • Device Information: Device model, iOS version, and app version for troubleshooting and compatibility.
  • Crash Reports: Anonymous crash data to help us fix bugs and improve stability.

2.3 Health Data (Optional)

With your explicit permission, NightFast may read and write the following HealthKit data:

  • Read: Sleep analysis data to provide insights about eating and sleep patterns
  • Write: Dietary energy (calories), protein, carbohydrates, fat, and caffeine intake

Health data is stored on your device and in your personal HealthKit database. We do not have access to this data on our servers.

4. Data Storage and Security

4.1 iCloud Storage

Your nutrition data is stored in your personal iCloud account using Apple's CloudKit service. This means:

  • Data is encrypted in transit and at rest
  • Only you can access your data through your Apple ID
  • Data syncs automatically across your Apple devices
  • We cannot access your data without your device

4.2 On-Device Processing

Sensitive features like food photo recognition use on-device machine learning. Photos are never transmitted to external servers.

4.3 Security Measures

  • End-to-end encryption for data in transit
  • Apple's secure enclave for authentication
  • Regular security audits and updates
  • Minimal data collection principle

NightFast integrates with the following third-party services:

You can opt out of optional analytics in Settings > Privacy.

6. Your Rights

You have the following rights regarding your personal data:

6.1 Access

Request a copy of all data we have about you. You can export your data from Settings > Privacy > Export My Data.

6.2 Correction

Update or correct inaccurate information directly in the App.

6.3 Deletion

Request deletion of all your data. Go to Settings > Account > Delete Account. This will permanently remove all your data from our systems and cannot be undone.

6.4 Data Portability

Export your data in a machine-readable format (JSON) from Settings > Privacy > Export My Data.

6.5 Opt-Out

Disable optional data collection (analytics) in Settings > Privacy.

7. Data Retention

  • Active Accounts: Data is retained as long as your account is active.
  • Deleted Accounts: Data is permanently deleted within 30 days of account deletion.
  • Analytics Data: Anonymous analytics are retained for up to 2 years.
  • Crash Reports: Retained for up to 90 days.

8. Children's Privacy

NightFast is not intended for children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe we have collected information from a child under 13, please contact us immediately.

9. International Data Transfers

Your data is stored in Apple's iCloud infrastructure, which may involve data centers in various countries. Apple ensures appropriate safeguards are in place for international data transfers.

10. California Privacy Rights (CCPA)

California residents have additional rights under the CCPA:

  • Right to know what personal information is collected
  • Right to delete personal information
  • Right to opt-out of the sale of personal information
  • Right to non-discrimination for exercising privacy rights

We do not sell your personal information.

11. European Privacy Rights (GDPR)

Users in the European Economic Area have rights under GDPR including access, rectification, erasure, restriction, portability, and objection. Contact us to exercise these rights.

12. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes through the App or by email. Your continued use of the App after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
